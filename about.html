About me

If you want to know more about me, read on.
I have ever since my early teens been interested in computers and technology. When I was 12 years old, I got my first computer, an Commodore Amiga 500.
By that time, all my time in front of the computer was spent on playing games, but as the years went by, I got more and more curious about how the computer worked and how software was made. When I began at the gymnasium (high school) I chose to study electronics, with focus on computers. I got to know more about a computer's hardware, but I also got my first contact with programming.
During this time, programming languages like BASIC, Pascal and Assembler were popular, and I learned the basics of coding in these languages. This was also the time when Internet became more popular and became every man's property.
Everybody was buying a modem and started subscriptions with ISP's, and so did I. This was how I found interest for HTML and it didn't take long until I created my first simple website.

After high school I was participating in an EU project so I was living in London, England for a year.
After returning to Sweden, I started a new course learning computer networking and worked much with Windows NT Server and later Windows 2000.
Unfortately the market for network administrators was filled by the time I was finished and I had to work with other stuff that was far away from computers for a long time.

During this time my family life started so with a wife and two kids I didn't have much time to focus on my interest for computers and programming.
However, now when my oldest son has become a teenager he has shown the same interest for computers as I had when I was young, so now I have the opportunity to share my interest with him and this also has made me come back to my passion for coding. So the latest year I have been studying programming on my own, primary in Python but also the basics in all three C lanuguages (C, C# and C++).
Not long after this I felt an urge that this is what I want to work with, and I also found out that there was a need for fullstack web developers. So I started to learn web development, both frontend and backend.
Everything I have learned this far has fallen naturally easy for me and I have enjoyed every minute of it.
As a Python programmer and a fullstack web developer I feel that I have found my calling in life and I am excited to see where this journey will take me.
I am eager to start work with this professionally and I am open for any opportunities that may come my way.
